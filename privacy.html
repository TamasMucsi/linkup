LinkUp Community Policy & Guidelines

Last updated: October 9, 2025

Welcome to LinkUp, a community-driven space where people come together to share stories, links, ideas, and conversations. To keep LinkUp open, welcoming, and safe for everyone, we’ve built a few ground rules. By using LinkUp, you agree to follow these policies, along with our [Terms of Service] and [Privacy Policy].

1. Community Guidelines

LinkUp thrives on respect, curiosity, and creativity. Here’s what’s expected of every member:

1.1 Respect Others

  • Be kind. No harassment, hate speech, threats, or bullying.
  • Do not post content that discriminates based on race, gender, sexuality, religion, nationality, or disability.
  • Attack ideas, not people.

1.2 Keep It Real

  • Don’t spam, self-promote excessively, or post repetitive content.
  • Don’t use bots or fake accounts to manipulate votes, trends, or engagement.
  • Don’t impersonate other users, public figures, or LinkUp staff.

1.3 Follow the Law

  • Do not post, share, or encourage illegal activities.
  • No pirated materials, stolen content, or anything that violates copyright law.
  • Never share private or identifying information about others without consent (“doxxing”).

1.4 NSFW & Sensitive Content

  • NSFW (Not Safe For Work) posts are allowed in designated communities only — and must be clearly labeled.
  • NSFW content must not include nudity involving minors, sexual exploitation, or non-consensual acts.
  • Communities can choose to disallow NSFW material entirely.
